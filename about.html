What is Budgeting?

Budgeting is the process of planning how to spend your money. It helps you understand your income and expenses.

It also helps you manage your money wisely, track your expenses,and plan for futer financial goals.

Importance of Budgeting

  • Controls overspending
  • Encourages saving
  • Reduces financial stress
  • Helps achieve financial goals
